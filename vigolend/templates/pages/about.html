About Us

Lendy was founded in 2010 as the second peer-to-peer lending marketplace in
the United States. Since then, Lendy has facilitated more
than $3.5 billion in loans to more than 180,000 people.

Through Lendy, people can invest in each other in a way that is financially and socially
rewarding. Borrowers apply online for a fixed-rate, fixed-term loan between $2,000
and $40,000. Individuals and institutions can invest in the loans and earn attractive
returns. Lendy handles all loan servicing on behalf of the matched borrowers and
investors.

Lendy Marketplace is backed by leading investors including Sequoia Capital, Francisco
Partners, Institutional Venture Partners, and Credit Suisse NEXT Fund.
